Junji Ito

"I am a horror maniac who prefers to stay at home"

Timeline

  • 1963 - Born in Sakashita, Japan (now Nakatsugawa, Gifu, Japan)
  • 1984 - Begins creating manga as a hobby in 1984, while working as a dental technician
  • 1987 - Itō's first published work, Tomie, begins serialization
  • 1992 - The first film adaptation of Itō's work is released
  • 1998 - Uzumaki begins serialization
  • 2001 - Gyo begins serialization
  • 2006 - Itō marries Ishiguro Ayako
  • 2019 - Itō receives an Eisner Award for his adaptation of Frankenstein

More information can be found on Itō's Wikipedia page.
